Project Overview

Transforming African Last-Mile Delivery

X-ENERGI is a clean-tech platform focused on sustainable last-mile delivery in Africa. We offer a dual business model of "Rider Franchise" and "Solar Charging Station Franchise" for riders and logistics networks.

Our solution addresses the critical challenges faced by delivery riders while capitalizing on the growing demand for eco-friendly logistics solutions across the continent. With our innovative approach, we're creating a sustainable ecosystem that benefits riders, businesses, and the environment.

Business Models

Two distinct franchise opportunities for investment and partnership

Rider Franchise

Rent-to-Own

3-Year Contract

Sign a 3-year contract to rent an electric motorcycle at a low threshold. Vehicle ownership transfers to the rider upon contract expiration.

  • Low entry threshold
  • Vehicle ownership after 3 years
  • Full operational support
  • Access to battery swap network
  • Priority for delivery partnerships

Solar Charging Station

Joint Construction

30-Vehicle Threshold

Units/teams with 30 electric motorcycles can join to co-build solar charging stations and share the revenue from battery swapping business.

  • 30-vehicle minimum threshold
  • Revenue sharing from battery swaps
  • Solar-powered infrastructure
  • Technical and operational training
  • Exclusive territory rights
